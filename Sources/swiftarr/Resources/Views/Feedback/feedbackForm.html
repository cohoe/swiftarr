  • Notes on This Form
  • If your event was the sort that had lots of people coming and going while it was running, we're most interested in the largest number of people that were there at any one time. The total number of people who showed up is useful too, if you want to include it.
  • For the "How did everything go?" question, if you were a reporter covering this event for the JoCo Daily News, what you you say about it?
  • For the "Issues" question: did the AV equipment work? Was there too much noise from nearby areas? Did extreme boatiness get in the way of your event? (No really. If this happens we want to know).
